Story:

Set in the Golden Age of Piracy (1715), Assassin's Creed IV: Black Flag follows Edward Kenway, a charming but reckless pirate captain with dreams of fortune and fame. As he sails through the lawless Caribbean, Edward becomes entangled in the ancient war between Assassins and Templars — two secret factions fighting for control over the future of humanity. From the bustling ports of Havana and Nassau to the hidden coves of remote islands, Edward’s journey takes him across a vast open world filled with naval battles, treasure hunting, and historical legends like Blackbeard and Charles Vane. Torn between his own ambitions and the larger cause of freedom, Edward must decide what kind of legacy he wants to leave behind. A tale of betrayal, freedom, and redemption, Black Flag delivers a gripping pirate saga at sea — with the heart of an Assassin.
